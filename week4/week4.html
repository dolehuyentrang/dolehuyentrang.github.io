CONAN EDOGAWA

Detective comic series

Certainly!"Detective Conan," also known as "Case Closed" in English, is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Aoyama Gosho. It has captured the hearts of many young fans not only in Japan but also in Vietnam. The story revolves around a young detective named Conan Edogawa, formerly known as Shinichi Kudo. Shinichi Kudo is a brilliant high school detective who, during an investigation, is attacked and forced to ingest a poison that transforms him into a child. He takes on the alias Conan Edogawa and hides his true identity while continuing to solve various cases. With the help of Professor Agasa, a brilliant inventor, Conan uses his deductive skills to uncover the truth behind each case he encounters. Conan's ultimate goal is to find the mysterious organization responsible for his condition and regain his original body. The series features intricate and captivating storylines, with numerous cases and the ongoing pursuit of the criminal organization.The "Detective Conan" manga and anime have gained a massive following worldwide, including in Vietnam. The series has captivated audiences with its intriguing mysteries, compelling characters, and the ongoing development of the overarching storyline. If you're interested, I would recommend exploring the manga or watching the anime to delve deeper into the rich and exciting world of "Detective Conan."
